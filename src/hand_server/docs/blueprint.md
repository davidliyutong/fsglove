### FPC Connector

The fpc connectors are arranged as follows

- Left Hand
    ```text
    ---------------------
       palm  |  middle
    ---------------------
      index  |  ring
    ---------------------
      thumb  |  little
    ---------------------
    ```

- Right Hand
  ```text
    ---------------------
       palm  |  middle
    ---------------------
       ring  |  index
    ---------------------
     little  |  thumb
    ---------------------
    ```
